## Observations ##
- true generative distribution: VonMises with $\mu = 0, \kappa =  1$ 
- number of observations $N_{obs} = 100$ 

## Model ##
Von Mises mixture model with up to 5 components
- mean $\mu$ evenly spaced between $0$ and $2\pi$
- concentration $\kappa$ randomly drawn from $U(0, K)$ with $K$ the number of components

- leak $\lambda = 0.1$
